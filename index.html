Welcome!

This website is the data companion to the paper Correlation Surfaces Associated to Zeros of Automorphic L-functions by Debmayla Basak, Cruz Castillo and Alexandru Zaharescu.

In this website, you will find a description of our data, visualizations related to pair and triple correlation of the zeros of certain $L$-functions, and tables of moments. Simply click on the corresponding tab above to see the data. For example, to the left is a graph of the discrete approximation of the Pair Correlation Surface.

For example, see a discrete approximation of the Pair Correlation Surface to the left. Our data makes heavy use of the zeros of $\zeta$ found on LMFDB which were computed by Platt(Add citation). For the other $L$-functions, we required the use of Rubinstein's $L$-function calculator lcalc(Add Citation).
