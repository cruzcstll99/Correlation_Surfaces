Welcome!

This website is the data companion to the paper Surfaces Associated to Zeros of Automorphic L-functions[1] by Debmayla Basak, Cruz Castillo and Alexandru Zaharescu. Here is a link to download the preprint. It is recommended to have a copy of the paper on hand while going looking through this website.

In this website, you will find a description of our data, visualizations related to the pair and the triple correlation of the zeros of certain $L$-functions, and tables of moments. Simply click on the corresponding tab above to see the data. For example, to the left is an animation of the discrete approximation of the Pair Correlation Surface generated from the zeros of $L(s,\Delta).$

For example, see a discrete approximation of the Pair Correlation Surface to the left. Our data makes heavy use of the zeros of $\zeta(s)$ found on LMFDB[2] which were computed by Platt [6]. For the other $L$-functions, we required the use of Rubinstein's $L$-function calculator lcalc[7].
